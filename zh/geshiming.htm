Biography

Shiming Ge is an Associate Professor with the Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences. He is also the member of Youth Innovation Promotion Association, Chinese Academy of Sciences. Prior to that, he was a senior researcher and project manager in Shanda Innovations, a researcher in Samsung Electronics and Nokia Research Center. He received the B.S. and Ph.D degrees both in Electronic Engineering from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2003 and 2008, respectively. His research mainly focuses on computer vision, data analysis, machine learning and AI security, especially efficient learning solutions toward scalable applications. He is a senior member of IEEE, CSIG and CCF. (Lab Website: https://imsg.ac.cn/).
